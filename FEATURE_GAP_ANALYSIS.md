## Executive Summary

OneLife is a solid foundation for a screen time management app built on Apple's native FamilyControls framework. However, to become a **world-class app** that helps users "get their lives back," significant feature gaps must be addressed.

The screen time app market in 2026 is **highly competitive** with 10+ serious players, each with unique innovations:
